# 🎮 Chocobaddie - Erlin's Island Adventure

A thrilling third-person survival adventure game where you play as **Erlin**, a 22-year-old girl who finds herself stranded on a mysterious island after jumping from an airplane. Navigate through forests, hunt animals, manage your survival stats, and escape island by island!

## 🎯 Game Overview

**Character:** Erlin, age 22 - An explorer and trekker

**Story:** After an unexpected airplane jump, Erlin lands on a mysterious island. She must survive in the wilderness, hunting animals for food, and after 3 days, find the boat to escape to the next island. Navigate through 5 islands to complete her adventure.

## 🎮 Controls

- **W/A/S/D** - Move forward, left, backward, right
- **SPACE** - Jump
- **MOUSE** - Look around (rotate camera)
- **LEFT CLICK** - Attack animals
- **INVENTORY CLICK** - Eat food to restore hunger

## 📊 Survival Mechanics

### Health System
- **Health:** Decreases when hungry or attacked by animals
- **Hunger:** Decreases over time during movement and activity
- **Energy:** Decreases with movement and combat, recovers at rest

### Food Chain
Hunt and eat different animals to survive:
- **Boar** - Common, good meat
- **Deer** - Fast, nutritious meat
- **Wolf** - Dangerous, high-quality meat

### Daily Cycle
- Game time progresses (6:00 AM to next day)
- After 3 days on each island, the boat becomes available
- Navigate through 5 islands to complete the adventure

## 🌍 Game Features

- **Dynamic Island Generation** - Procedurally generated forests and wildlife
- **Real-time Day/Night Cycle** - Time progression affects gameplay
- **Animal AI** - Realistic animal behavior with hunting patterns
- **Inventory System** - Collect and manage food items
- **Progressive Difficulty** - More islands with enhanced challenges
- **Third-Person Perspective** - Full 360° camera control with mouse
